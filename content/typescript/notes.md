title: Notes on Typescript
---

- **Pick** `Pick<type, keys>`
Pick keys only from type.

- **Omit** `Omit<type, “key1” | “key2”>` **opposite Pick**
Pick all from *type* exclude *keys*.

- **Record** `Record<keys, type>`
Construct object with keys and values of type *type*.
